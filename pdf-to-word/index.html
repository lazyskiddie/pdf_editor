📗 PDF to Word

Extract all text from your PDF and download it as an editable Word-compatible (.rtf) document. Everything runs in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

📗

Drop PDF here or click to browse

No sign-up required  ·  Fast and private

📋 What this tool does:

Extracts all text content from each page of your PDF using the PDF.js engine and saves it as an RTF file, which opens in Microsoft Word, LibreOffice, and Google Docs. Images and complex formatting are not preserved — only text. All processing is 100% local.
